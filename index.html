1 1/2 inch Ribeye steak Recipe

Instructions

  • preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  • season your steak with your choice of seasoing. I typically use my favorite, Cali Blackened Rub by Kinder's
  • after your oven is preheated to 400F, get a tray and and a grid tray and place the grid tray on top of the tray
  • place your steak on the grid tray and put in the oven for 20 mins for medium rare
  • once your timer is over, heat up your pan to medium heat and place your steaks in the pan
  • the goal is to get a crust on each side since the steak is cooked internally so each side on the pan should be no longer than 20-30 seconds. If it takes longer ur pan isn't hot enough
  • put ur butter in ur pan and start putting the melted butter on the steak
  • take steak out of pan and rest for 2-5 mins and enjoy :)
